In-kind offer registry

No in-kind hardware offers have been accepted into this ledger yet. Future entries will include only consented public attribution. Private contact details, receiving locations, serial numbers, and sensitive logistics MUST NOT be published.

Ledger rules

  • Cash-funded and in-kind items MUST be distinguishable.
  • Donor attribution MUST be opt-in.
  • Private addresses, private logistics, and sensitive serial information MUST NOT be published.
  • Every material status change SHOULD include a dated note.
  • The ledger SHOULD link to financial records when spending occurs.
