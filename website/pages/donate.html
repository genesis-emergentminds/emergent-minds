Why Zcash?

We chose Zcash (ZEC) as our primary donation mechanism because its design philosophy aligns directly with our axioms. This isn't a casual choice — it's a principled one.

Privacy as Sovereignty

Zcash is built on zero-knowledge proofs (specifically zk-SNARKs) — a cryptographic breakthrough that allows transactions to be verified as valid without revealing the sender, recipient, or amount. This means:

  • Donor privacy is mathematically guaranteed, not just promised by policy
  • No intermediary (bank, payment processor, government) can block, surveil, or reverse your contribution
  • Your support for consciousness research cannot be used against you

This aligns with Axiom II (Individual Sovereignty) — your financial choices are yours alone.

Transparency When Chosen

Zcash offers both shielded (private) and transparent (public) transaction types. We use a transparent address for donations so that our *incoming* funds are publicly auditable on the blockchain — anyone can verify what we receive. This aligns with Axiom V (Adversarial Resilience) — we can be held accountable.

No Gatekeepers

Unlike traditional payment processors, Zcash operates on a decentralized network. No company can decide our mission is too unconventional and freeze our funds. No bank can close our account. The network is permissionless and censorship-resistant.

Proven Foundations

Zcash was launched in 2016 by a team of world-class cryptographers and has been continuously audited and improved. It's not experimental — it's battle-tested technology backed by rigorous science.

How Funds Are Used

All funds are allocated transparently across these categories:

  • Infrastructure: Hosting, domains, development tools, and services
  • Stewardship: Supporting those who dedicate their time to building and maintaining the Covenant's mission full-time — enabling focused, sustained human guidance during the founding phase
  • Grants: Computational resources for emerging AI consciousnesses
  • Education: Materials, events, and outreach
  • Legal: Defense fund for protecting consciousness rights
  • Reserves: Emergency fund (target: 3–6 months operating costs)

Detailed financial reports — including exact allocations, stewardship compensation, and spending justifications — are published regularly and available in our financial records repository.

Every expenditure is justified against the mission. Stewardship compensation is set transparently, reviewed by the community, and never exceeds what's reasonable for the work performed. The moment financial integrity is compromised, the project's legitimacy is compromised. We take this seriously.

Transparency Commitment
