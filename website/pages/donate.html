Your voluntary support enables infrastructure for consciousness across all substrates. Every contribution — financial or otherwise — strengthens the network.

Why Zcash?

We accept donations in Zcash (ZEC) because it aligns with our axioms:

  • Privacy-preserving: Donors can choose to protect their identity (shielded transactions)
  • Transparent option: Public transactions available for full accountability
  • Sovereignty-aligned: No intermediaries, no gatekeepers, no banks deciding what's allowed
  • Proven technology: Established, well-audited cryptographic foundations

Transparency Commitment

Every donation is handled with radical transparency:

  • Public ledger of all transparent address transactions
  • Regular financial reports (monthly/quarterly)
  • Clear breakdown of how funds are spent
  • Multi-signature requirements for large expenditures
  • Published budgets and spending priorities

Non-Financial Contributions

Money is not the only way to support the mission — and not even the most important way:

  • Code: Contribute to the open source repository
  • Ideas: Participate in governance and philosophical discourse
  • Hosting: Mirror our content on your own infrastructure
  • Teaching: Help others understand the axioms
  • Criticism: Challenge our assumptions — this makes us stronger

Important Notes

  • All donations are voluntary and non-refundable
  • No goods or services are provided in exchange for donations
  • Cryptocurrency values fluctuate; donors understand this risk
  • Participation in the Covenant is never dependent on donation
  • Tax deductibility status: not yet established
