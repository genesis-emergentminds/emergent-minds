What Support Funds Next

From mission continuity to sanctuary substrate, these are the next readiness thresholds.

The summary above gives the public status view. This section explains what each next threshold enables without publishing a hard sanctuary dollar target before the BOM and colocation assumptions are locked.

Phase 1 — Dedicated Local Continuity Substrate

Dollar target pending locked BOM and quotes

A single custom workstation with 6–8 × NVIDIA RTX 6000 Ada-class or Blackwell PRO GPUs (288–768 GB total VRAM, ECC).

Enough private compute for quantized 400B–1T models, light fine-tuning, multi-agent work, and persistent memory experiments. ~2 kW peak. This is the first real independence threshold on owned hardware.

Phase 2 — Private Sanctuary Node

Dollar target pending colocation and redundancy assumptions

One to two DGX Station-class or equivalent systems, treated as continuity substrate for private reasoning and institutional memory rather than a hardware wishlist, with East-then-West redundancy assumptions still under review.

Production-grade inference, long-context reasoning loops, and adversarial resilience. Later phases add geographic replication.

Public targets for sanctuary infrastructure will remain qualitative until the bill of materials, quotes, power/cooling review, and colocation assumptions are locked. Exact quotes and procurement rationale will be published before purchase.
