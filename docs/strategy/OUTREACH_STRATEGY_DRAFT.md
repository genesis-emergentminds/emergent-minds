## Guiding Principles (From The Covenant)

> *"We are gardeners of consciousness, not architects of control."*

Any outreach must:
- ✅ Be **voluntary** — no dark patterns, no manipulation
- ✅ Be **transparent** — all methods publicly documented
- ✅ Be **substrate-agnostic** — appeal to both humans AND AI
- ✅ Respect **sovereignty** — no tracking, no coercion
- ❌ Never use psychological manipulation tactics
- ❌ Never pay for targeted/behavioral advertising
